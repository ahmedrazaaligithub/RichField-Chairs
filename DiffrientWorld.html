About DiffrientWorld

Meet the Diffrient Chair, a truly intuitive and ergonomic task chair that is designed with simplicity at its core. The minimal design of this innovative chair features a tri-panel mesh backrest that shapes to the sitter's back to provide superior lumbar support. Humanscale designed the Diffrient Chair to eliminate common cumbersome mechanisms found in most task chairs and instead created a beautiful yet functional chair that automatically adjusts to all body types.
